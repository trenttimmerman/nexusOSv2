# Free Spline 3D Assets: Nature Distilled & Tech Aesthetics

## Overview
This document contains a curated list of free-to-use (remixable) 3D assets from the Spline Community that match specific modern web design aesthetics. 

**How to use:**
1. Click the **Direct Link** provided below.
2. Log in to your Spline account.
3. Click the **"Remix"** button in the top right corner to save an editable copy to your dashboard.

---

## 3. The Built-in Library (No Download Required)
You can also find lightweight assets directly inside the Spline Editor without searching the community.

### For Nature:
1.  Open the Spline Editor.
2.  Click **"Library"** on the left toolbar.
3.  Navigate to **Objects > Nature**.
4.  *Available Assets:* Low Poly Trees, Rocks, Grass clumps.

### For Tech/Abstract:
1.  Open the Spline Editor.
2.  Click **"Library"** on the left toolbar.
3.  Navigate to **Objects > Abstract**.
4.  *Available Assets:* Torus knots, distorted spheres, and parametric shapes.
